How it works

It is an online library when you can book a book. At first, find a book and fill the form. We will prepare a book for you.

After that you can go to our library and take this book. You can use the book for 30 days and after have to return it, or you will get a fine.

Before you go to return a book, please, fill the form again and come on the same day to the library.

It will help you to understand before that do you have a fine or not.

If you lost a book, we have the form for that too which will tell you how much do you have to pay for this book.

Thank you for using our app!
